Caipirinha de Cachaça

A drink typical Brazil with cachaça, sugars, ice and lime (maybe lemon? Who know!)


Ingrediente

  • 1 Vegas shot glass cachaça de Minas
  • 2 cups clean water, frozen please
  • 2 lime, slice and slice again
  • 2 tablespoons sugars

Steps:

  1. Put lime in glass with sugar and cachaça
  2. Smash lime in glass with thick short peg
  3. Insert pre-frozen water
  4. Pour cachaça over
  5. and... enjoy!
