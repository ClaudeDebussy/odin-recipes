Pão de Queijo

A very tasty bread of cheeses! You need to try this!


Ingrediente

  • 1 sack of pão de queijo cheese powders
  • .5 to 1 cups clean water

Steps:

  1. Open sack of cheese powders and add clean water
  2. Follow instructions on back of sack
  3. Bake in oven
  4. and... enjoy!
