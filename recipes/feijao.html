Feijão com arroz

A plate typical Brazilian dish white rice and bean, with some farofa side.


Ingrediente

  • 2 cups white rice
  • 2 cups clean water
  • 2 teaspoons garlic, minced please
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced please
  • 6-12 bay leaf, depending on size of bay leaf
  • 1-2 teaspoons of salts

Steps:

  1. Make the bean.
  2. Make the mush onion.
  3. Join the bean and the mush onion.
  4. Make the rices.
  5. Join the bean with the rices and... enjoy!
